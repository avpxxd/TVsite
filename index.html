Tips & Tricks
Keyboard Navigation

Use the arrow keys to move through menus, rows, and cards. Press Enter to open anything, and Esc to go back.

Open Detail View

Click or press Enter on any title card to see its trailer, full cast, ratings, and exactly where to stream it.

Filter by Service

Open Settings to choose your streaming subscriptions and region — the app will only show content you can actually watch.

Search Anything

Head to the Search tab to find movies and shows instantly. Type with the on-screen keyboard or just start typing on your physical keyboard.

Browse by Category

Switch between the Movies and Shows tabs for category rows tailored to each content type.

Fresh Content Every Visit

The home screen loads a different selection of trending and popular titles every time the app opens — there's always something new to discover.
